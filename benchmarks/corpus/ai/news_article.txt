NovaBridge AI Raises $85 Million in Series B to Disrupt Enterprise Analytics

SAN FRANCISCO — NovaBridge AI, a fast-growing startup specializing in AI-powered enterprise analytics, announced today that it has secured $85 million in Series B funding led by Crestview Ventures, with participation from several prominent institutional investors.

The company, which offers an innovative solution for real-time data intelligence, is poised to disrupt the rapidly growing market for enterprise decision-making tools. Founded in 2023, NovaBridge has experienced remarkable growth, with revenue increasing by 300% year-over-year and its customer base expanding to over 200 enterprise clients.

"This funding will enable us to accelerate product development, expand our go-to-market strategy, and scale our world-class team," said CEO Maria Chen. "We are uniquely positioned to address the growing demand for intelligent, actionable insights in today's data-driven landscape."

Industry analysts have noted that the enterprise analytics market is projected to reach $45 billion by 2028, representing a significant opportunity for emerging players. With this latest round of funding, NovaBridge is well-capitalized to capture market share and solidify its position as a category leader in the space.
